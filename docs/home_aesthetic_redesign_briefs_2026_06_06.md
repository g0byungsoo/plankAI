# Home aesthetic redesign — 3 minimalistic luxury fitness designer briefs

**Date:** 2026-06-06 (founder feedback round 4)
**Trigger:** Founder asked for more visual design on Home:
> "we need more visual design and let's spawn minimalistic luxury designers for ios fitness apps for weight loss for gen-z women and try to design it better aesthetically for home."

**Goal:** Lift Home from "functional component stack" to "designed page." 3 POVs spawned: Lasta/Noom (WL-specific), Strava-2024 / Apple Fitness+ (iOS aesthetic hero), Flo / Stripes / Alloy (femme-luxury wellness).

---

## Cross-brief verdict

### Unanimous diagnosis
Home reads as a **stack of designed components**, not a **designed page**. Six modules competing at roughly equal visual weight (workout shouts via scrapbook chrome; lesson + weight talk via hairlines; everything else feels like a list). The eye lands nowhere first. No hero. No still-life / aesthetic substrate. Mixed chrome registers (scrapbook + hairlines + capsules + soft cards) = "design system mid-migration."

### Unanimous lifts (different angles, same conclusion)
1. **One typographic / aesthetic hero moment** — Lasta calls it the "weather report opener," Apple-Fitness calls it the "metric badge stack," Flo calls it "the reading." All three are the same move: a designed top-of-screen moment that pulls from HER data ("yesterday was *gentle*. today, your call ♥" / "this week, you *showed up* 3 times ♥").
2. **Tier the cocoa pill** — currently ALL CTAs are cocoa pills at equal weight. Three voices arguing for: hero pill solid cocoa-100, secondary pill cocoa outline on cream, tertiary text-only with hairline underline.
3. **JeweledRose earns ONE job per scroll** — currently scattered (sparkline, week dots, deltas, accents). All three brief writers said: restraint = luxury. Use it on ONE thing (the trend signal / the becoming direction) and nothing else.
4. **Vertical rhythm reset** — currently mixed 16/20/24pt gutters. One brief said push hero gutters to 28pt (vs 16pt utility), another said unify to 20pt. Consensus: pick ONE rhythm token and enforce it.

### Where they disagreed (real forks)

**Photography substrate (still-life moment):**
- **Lasta brief**: ADD a tactile still-life (linen folds, ceramic, peony) at ~120pt full-bleed behind/beside lesson card. "Single biggest premium-warm vs clinical lever."
- **Strava/Apple brief**: NO photography. Cohort body-comparison reflex is a risk. Use existing signature stickers at canonical 72pt instead.
- **Flo brief**: Add 120pt photographic/illustrated still-life — but **NEVER a body**. Pressed flowers, ceramic, abstract gradient. Rotates weekly, never daily.

→ **2 of 3 want a still-life moment**, all 3 agree NO body imagery. The "still life as substrate" is on the table; the question is photography vs sticker-scaled-up.

**The hero choice:**
- **Lasta brief**: Add a typographic hero ABOVE the lesson card (no chrome, generous padding). Keep lesson/workout as actionable cards underneath.
- **Strava brief**: Pick ONE actionable card per scroll based on engagement-day state (lesson if day 3, workout if day 30). The OTHER one shrinks. Never both at hero volume.
- **Flo brief**: Promote a slim TREND card to position 1 (Fraunces Light 48pt delta, jeweledRose if goalward, no CTA). This becomes the hero. "Biggest type is the metric the cohort actually opens the app for."

→ **3 different opinions on what should be the hero.** Founder's pick determines everything downstream.

**Sticker placement:**
- **Lasta**: bowSatin top-right of lesson hero 32pt, cherries bottom-left of weight card 24pt, sparkleGlossy beside today's-lesson cocoa pill.
- **Strava**: ONE sticker per scroll (flower3D on lesson days, sparkleGlossy on workout days) at canonical 72pt zero rotation. Hero-card top-right.
- **Flo**: Stickers stay decorative punctuation only; the real "hero photography" is a 120pt still-life on pageIvory.

→ Sticker placement is mostly aligned: 1 per scroll, canonical size, hero-card top-right. The size differs (Lasta 32pt vs Strava 72pt).

**Type ramp:**
- **Lasta**: Fraunces Light 28pt italic-punch hero zone above lesson + Fraunces Light 48pt module label "*today*" above weight digit. Two new Fraunces moments.
- **Strava**: Keep Fraunces ONLY at hero scale (28pt+); switch SF Pro Text for ALL micro-meta under 14pt (timestamps, "day 12 of becoming," sparkline labels). "Fraunces feels chosen again instead of defaulted-to."
- **Flo**: No specific type ramp call — focus is on hero composition.

→ The SF Pro Text swap for micro-meta is opinionated and only one voice argued for it. Worth a separate decision.

---

## Recommended synthesis (path of least regret)

**Core moves all 3 agree on (lock these):**
1. **Hierarchy fix** — one actionable hero per scroll, others shrink
2. **Tier the cocoa pill** — solid cocoa hero, outline cocoa secondary, text-only tertiary
3. **JeweledRose restraint** — one job per scroll (the trend/direction signal)
4. **Vertical rhythm token** — unify gutters
5. **One signature sticker per scroll** at canonical size, hero-card placement

**Forks for founder:**
- **Still-life moment?** Add a 120pt non-body still-life slot above lesson (Lasta+Flo) OR scale a signature sticker as the visual hero (Strava)?
- **Hero choice?** Typographic "reading" line at top (Lasta), engagement-day-dependent action hero (Strava), or trend-as-hero stat tile (Flo)?
- **SF Pro Text for micro-meta under 14pt?** Yes (Strava) or stay all-Fraunces (Lasta + Flo)?
