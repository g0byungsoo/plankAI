## 1. The 3 convergent findings (all 3 experts independently arrived at)

These are the load-bearing rules. Every downstream rule is a mechanic for keeping these true.

1. **Cream is the sheet of paper; photos are stamps adhered to it; stickers are stickers on top of stamps.** Real photo ≥40% of hero canvas, stickers ≤10%, max 2 stickers per screen.

2. **AI-generated 3D plastic stickers die permanently.** Replace with photographed real objects (a real bow, a real flower, a real charm) shot on cream paper, cut out, drop-shadowed. her75 IMG_6275's "matcha + lily + book stack" is THIS technique, not illustration.

3. **Photography is curated/commissioned, never licensed stock.** Sources: Are.na boards, Death to Stock, Stocksy curated, individual photographer Instagram (Beth Kirby, Carla Lalli Music, Athena Calderone, Aran Goyoaga). NEVER Unsplash, NEVER Shutterstock, NEVER "diverse-stock-women-with-salad."

## 2. North Star compositions (lock these as references)

- **her75 IMG_6280 "Personalizing *your* space"** — pure typography on 80% cream canvas. THE BRAND NORTH STAR. Every Direction A hero should aspire to this level of restraint. When in doubt, remove an element.
- **her75 IMG_6275 "Matching *your* energy"** — 3-photo collage (matcha + lily + faceless mirror selfie) anchoring a Fraunces hero + cocoa social-proof pill. THE COLLAGE TEMPLATE.
- **her75 IMG_6278 "day one" sticker on faceless cropped-shoulder mirror selfie** — THE PLAN-REVEAL TEMPLATE. Sticker layered ON photo, not next to it.

## 3. The locked photo rubric

### Subject palette — IN
- Hand-portraits holding ritual objects (peach, matcha, journal, ceramic mug, paperback)
- Faceless cropped-shoulder mirror selfies (1990s plastic phone optional)
- Single-object still lifes (one bouquet, one folded towel, one Owala bottle, one candle)
- Sloppy-real food plating (NOT food-styled magazine plates)
- Lived-in apartment fragments (linen sheets, sage candle on stack of books, light through curtains)
- Domestic ritual moments (matcha being whisked, journal being written in)

### Subject palette — REJECT
- Faces above the eyes (premium register hides identity, lets the user project)
- Bodies above the bra strap or below the hip — anywhere that triggers comparison
- Gym selfies, sports bras, kettlebells, dumbbells, workout machines
- Stanley cups (post-2024 dethroning; Owala took the slot — but use sparingly)
- Vital Proteins, Athletic Greens, any branded supplement
- Stock-coded "diverse women laughing with salad"
- AI-generated faces (the cohort spots them instantly)
- Before/after framing in any form
- "Wellness" abstract gradients, glassmorphism, neon glow
- 2022 Spotify Wrapped disco-ball / pastel-sparkle slop

### Color palette rules
- Photos must sit on cream `#FDF6F4` without fighting it
- Cream has +9 magenta cast — REJECT hot pink, fuchsia, lavender subjects (they fight the magenta)
- Black point clamped (no #000 in photos — darkest tone ~ cocoa `#3D2A2A`)
- Saturation -15, vibrance -10, blacks +15-25 in post
- 2-4% film grain over every photo
- Max 3 hue families per composition

### Composition rules
- Aspect: 1:1 or 4:5 ONLY — never 16:9
- 3-tier hierarchy per collage: anchor (200-240pt round / 280-340pt soft-rect), flankers (110-140pt edge-bleed), accents (36-48pt at ±6° tilt)
- Center-50% column stays clear (typography lives there)
- Cocoa social-proof pill bridges photo cluster + headline
- 2-3px `#FFFAF8` halo on cutout edges (the "paper stamp" lift)
- Axis-aligned photos; only stickers tilt (±3-6°)

### Sticker rules
- ON photos: 1 max, painterly only (NEVER line-art), 30-40% of photo's smaller dimension, ±3-6° tilt
- NEAR photos: 2 max, 36-48pt (NOT the 56-72pt structural size used when sticker is the hero)
- SEPARATE as typographic ornament: 24-32pt, 0° tilt
- Hard cap: 2 stickers per hero screen
- Sticker creep is the silent killer — codify the cap now

## 5. The 1-page photo sourcing brief (paste-ready)

```
PROJECT: JeniFit hero photo library — 30 photos
DELIVERY FORMAT: PNG, transparent or cream #FDF6F4 background, 1:1 or 4:5

SUBJECT PALETTE (accept):
- hand-portraits holding small objects (matcha, peach, journal, mug, paperback)
- faceless cropped-shoulder mirror selfies (always shoulder-down or chin-down, no full face)
- single-object still lifes (one bouquet, one folded towel, one ritual item)
- sloppy-real food plating (not magazine-styled)
- apartment fragments (linen, sage candle, light through curtains, journal page)

SUBJECT PALETTE (reject):
- any face above the eyes
- bodies above bra strap or below hip
- gym imagery, sports bras, kettlebells, workout machines
- stock-coded "diverse women laughing with salad"
- AI-generated faces (visible tells: too-smooth skin, weird hand fingers)
- before/after, scale photos, bathroom-scale imagery
- branded supplements, Stanley cups, kombucha bottles with visible labels

LIGHTING PALETTE:
- warm overcast OR golden-hour-without-flare
- NO clinical white softbox
- NO Instagram-filter saturation

COLOR PALETTE:
- harmonize with cream #FDF6F4, cocoa #3D2A2A, dusty rose #C4677A
- REJECT hot pink, fuchsia, lavender (fight the cream's magenta cast)
- max 3 hue families per shot
- clamp black point at ~cocoa, never #000

POST-PRODUCTION:
- saturation -15
- vibrance -10
- blacks +15 to +25
- 2-4% film grain overlay
- 2-3px #FFFAF8 halo on cutout edges (the "paper stamp" lift)

COMPOSITION:
- 1:1 or 4:5 ONLY — never 16:9
- subject in center-third
- generous negative space — at least 30% of canvas is cream
- single hero subject — never multi-subject grids

MIDJOURNEY / FLUX SEED PROMPT:
"single object on cream paper, faceless cropped-shoulder mirror selfie OR hand holding ceramic mug,
warm natural light, film grain, slight desaturation, no logos, no faces above the eyes,
no gym, no workout, no sports bra, no scale, no body-comparison, no AI-generated face,
shot on iPhone or Pentax K1000, 1:1 aspect, cream background #FDF6F4 ::
negative prompt: full face, abs, sports bra, gym, scale, weighing, before/after, smoothie,
salad bowl, kettlebell, Lululemon, gradient, glassmorphism, sparkle, disco ball,
plastic 3D sticker, AI face, fingers"

REVIEW CHECKLIST (7-point pass):
[ ] passes Goop test (would a Goop reader find this premium?)
[ ] passes Cereal magazine litmus (vs Hailey Bieber Instagram)
[ ] no face above eyes
[ ] sits on cream without fighting it
[ ] single hero subject
[ ] saturation cool enough to read as natural light
[ ] no stock-photography "tell"
```
