# JeniFit Positioning — Round 2 Deliverable

## TL;DR

**Verdict: The founder is 70% right and Round 1 was 60% wrong about the SURFACE, but the founder is wrong about three load-bearing specifics that will get JeniFit FTC-cited, Apple-rejected, or TikTok-banned within 90 days if shipped as quoted.**

Round 1 correctly identified the brand voice and the "food noise" mechanism, but mis-routed both as the **headline positioning** when they belong as the **product texture inside a cohort-led claim wrapper**. The founder correctly identified that pure-soft register at $48/yr is a wellness-app pricing trap and that the GLP-1 cohort transacts on effectiveness recognition before identity comfort. But the founder's word choice ("GLP-1 alternative," and the implicit "we get you results" register) walks directly into the NextMed enforcement zone, TikTok July 2025 policy, and Apple 5.2.1.

**The right answer is hybrid-throughout with cohort-led conviction in the noun and JeniFit brand voice in the verb** — leading with the **post-GLP-1 maintenance cohort** as the differentiated wedge no telehealth competitor can serve without cannibalizing their drug revenue.

**Revised primary positioning (App Store hero + paywall):**

> **JeniFit: Weight Care for the GLP-1 Era**
> *the keep-it-off app — for after the shot, beside it, or building the habits the drug never taught you.*

**Five things that MUST be true for this to work:**
1. The word "alternative" is deleted from every surface, replaced by "instead" / "without one" / "your own way" — and only ever inside the app, never in App Store metadata or paid creative.
2. The post-GLP-1 maintenance curriculum (12-week, JAMA-grounded) ships **before** App Store metadata changes — the conviction claim must have substantiating product, not vibes.
3. Cohort segmentation as onboarding screen 1 is the load-bearing UX change. Without it the conviction copy lies to the user inside 30 seconds.
4. Brand names (Ozempic, Wegovy, Mounjaro, Zepbound, semaglutide, tirzepatide) appear **only in user-generated creator content** where the user speaks them. App-controlled surfaces use "GLP-1" (class noun, generic, FTC-safe) or "the shot."
5. Conviction lives in the **noun phrase** ("Weight Care for the GLP-1 Era," "keep-it-off," "habit-built maintenance"); brand voice lives in the **verb phrase** ("hold," "keep," "becoming," italic-Fraunces accent). Not pick-one — co-present in every line.

## Major Findings

### What the round-2 evidence revealed

**The cohort wedge is real and Round 1 underweighted it.** Six independent expert reads converged on three quantitative anchors: RAND Aug 2025 puts ~12% of US adults on a GLP-1 with women at 15% (~19M); JAMA Jan 2025 (n=125,474) shows 47-65% discontinue within year 1; Lancet eClinicalMedicine 2026 meta-analysis shows 53-64% weight regained within 12 months post-discontinuation. **The post-GLP-1 maintenance cohort is the single most-mispriced wedge in consumer digital health right now** — Omada's wraparound-support data (0.8% one-year post-discontinuation weight change vs Lilly's SURMOUNT-4 placebo arm at 11-14% regain) is a 10-15x effect-size delta the cohort can be told about without any FTC exposure because it's a published third-party stat.

**Telehealth competitors structurally cannot serve this cohort at the bundle price.** Noom-Rx, WW Med+, Found, Sequence, Calibrate, Hims+Novo all monetize prescription access. Their business model REQUIRES the user stay on the drug. They lose the user the moment she discontinues. JeniFit's defensible moat is the 47-65% they shed by design. This is uncontested by anyone except Omada (B2B-employer only) and Virta (B2B-only).

**Conviction structure outconverts soft structure at the App Store + paywall surface in this category.** RevenueCat 2025: hard paywall users generate $3.09 RPI by day 14 vs $0.38 on soft freemium. Cal AI scaled to $40M ARR (acquired by MFP March 2026) on aggressive conviction structure + segmentation question + dynamic pricing. BetterMe's $2M/mo paywall opens with custom avatar + projected outcomes. Reverse Health dominates women-40+ on conviction-led copy. Noom GLP-1 Companion hit $100M ARR in 4 months on results-claiming positioning.

**BUT — conviction in this cohort is mechanism + cohort + relative outcome, NOT absolute weight numbers.** FTC NextMed final order (Dec 2025, $150K + 20-year fencing) penalized "53 lb / 23% body weight" claims. Any first-party numeric weight-loss promise from JeniFit is a consent decree waiting to happen. Survivable conviction language: "keep-it-off," "8x more likely with structure" (citable to Virta), "47% discontinue within a year" (citable to JAMA), "protect lean mass," "stimulate your own satiety signals." Dead language: "lose X lbs," "results like Wegovy," "GLP-1 alternative."

### Where the founder's intuition holds up

1. **Soft-only at acquisition is a wellness-app trap.** "Quiet your food noise" on the App Store does not click for a Cal-AI-trained US shopper who doesn't yet know food noise is her problem. Conviction noun-phrase is required at the App Store hero, paywall H1, and onboarding screen 1.

2. **Current $47.99 underprices the cohort.** GLP-1 users have been paying $300-1500/mo for the drug. $79-99/yr is a rounding error. Multiple experts (regulatory, cohort, copy, mechanism) converged on $79.99 annual as the right anchor. Founder pricing intuition is correct.

3. **The Round 1 "Quiet" composite winner was an editorial finding, not a conversion finding.** Round 1 panelists indexed on brand-voice protection. The 7-14% US paywall conversion is positioning fuzziness, not pricing — and not register.

4. **JeniFit's product (lessons + Becoming + Jeni + food rail + breathwork) is structurally closer to "behavioral GLP-1 companion + post-GLP-1 maintenance" than to "general weight loss app."** The 75%+ lesson completion vs 23% workouts is the empirical fingerprint of where PMF lives. The repositioning rides existing PMF, not greenfield.

### Where the founder is wrong or needs to thread differently

1. **"GLP-1 alternative" is the regulatory tripwire.** FDA sent 30 warning letters in Feb 2026 specifically for this phrase. Blackburn/Skrmetti July 2025 Senate letter asked FTC to investigate it. Meta + TikTok auto-reject creative containing it. Delete from internal vocabulary, replace with "instead" / "without one" / "your own way" — and only inside the app, never on App Store metadata or paid creative.

2. **"Conviction-then-soft as binary sequence" is the wrong mental model.** The right model is **hybrid in the same sentence**: conviction in the noun, soft in the verb. "**Hold your loss**" + italic-Fraunces accent. "**Weight Care** for the GLP-1 era." This is the Aesop/Glossier move — clinical noun wrapped in editorial verbs and typography. Pure conviction sounds like Noom (and the cohort has been burned by Noom's 1,200+ BBB cancellation complaints).

3. **Plank-as-workout doesn't deliver the muscle-preservation claim.** GLP-1 weight loss is 25-40% lean mass. The cohort reads Peter Attia, follows obesity-med Substacks, knows the difference between core endurance and progressive overload. Conviction muscle claims without a progressive resistance track ship a bait-and-switch that craters reviews. Either ship the resistance track first or pull muscle-preservation language until you do.

4. **"On-GLP-1" cannot be the lead acquisition cohort.** Without injection tracking, on-GLP-1 users install → churn within 48h. Shotsy's "estimated medication levels with peer-reviewed clinical data" is table-stakes conviction in that subcohort. Lead with **post-GLP-1**, serve on-GLP-1 secondarily, defer the dedicated on-GLP-1 push until injection-lite ships.

5. **"Soft language only matters after value recognition" is right at the gate, wrong as a universal principle.** The retention surface — day 3, day 30, day 90 — is overwhelmingly where soft voice wins. JeniFit's 75%+ lesson completion is the proof. Apply the founder's principle to the App Store and paywall; do **not** apply it inside the lessons, Becoming tab, Jeni voice, or notification copy. That's the engagement metric that's actually working.

### The specific subcohort to lead with

**LEAD: Post-GLP-1 (0-12 months off the drug).**

Five reasons converging across 6 of 7 expert reads:

1. **Largest underserved cohort.** 47-65% of GLP-1 users discontinue in year 1; ~4-8M US women in the trailing 12-month window with no app built for them.
2. **Highest willingness-to-pay.** They were paying $300-1500/mo for the drug they just lost or quit. $79-99/yr is rounding error.
3. **Highest pain → conversion intent.** Existential fear of regain (Lancet 2026: 53-64% regained within 12 months) drives subscription tenure.
4. **Lowest regulatory risk.** No drug-equivalence claims required. Maintenance science (JAMA + Virta + Omada) is publicly citable.
5. **Structurally unowned.** Noom-Rx, WW Med+, Found, Sequence cannot serve them without cannibalizing prescription revenue. Reverse Health is 40+ menopause-positioned, not GLP-1-fluent. Shotsy/MeAgain are on-GLP-1 trackers without maintenance mode.

**SECONDARY (in-app, served through cohort fork): on-GLP-1 companion** (muscle + food noise + protein floor) **and GLP-1-curious/can't-access** (behavioral mimetic protocol). Both served by the same product loop after the onboarding cohort question. Neither leads acquisition until injection-lite (on-GLP-1) ships and the "alternative" regulatory question is settled (GLP-1-curious).

## Disagreements Among Experts (unsmoothed)

The 7 round-2 reports broadly converged on cohort lead and pricing but split on five real fault lines:

**1. On-GLP-1 inclusion in lead positioning.**
The competitive-intel and copy-conversion experts wanted on-GLP-1 + post-GLP-1 to share the acquisition lead. The behavioral-mechanism and contrarian experts argued on-GLP-1 cannot honestly be lead-served without injection tracking + nausea-aware nutrition + side-effect logging, and would tank reviews within 30 days. **Resolution: post-GLP-1 leads acquisition; on-GLP-1 is served through the in-app cohort fork without dedicated paid creative until injection-lite ships in week 6-8.**

**2. Whether GLP-1-alternative cohort can be served at all.**
The cohort-deep-dive expert argued it's the largest TAM (KFF: half of GLP-1-curious cite affordability) and JeniFit should serve it with "less for the shot to do" softer framing. The regulatory and contrarian experts argued the phrase is non-negotiable and any positioning that even gestures at "replacement" walks into FDA/AG enforcement. **Resolution: serve the cohort, NEVER use the word "alternative" externally. Internal user-facing pill: "looking for results without the drug" or "building habits first." External pitch never names the substitution frame.**

**3. Register: conviction-then-soft vs. hybrid-throughout.**
The cohort-deep-dive, regulatory, and contrarian experts recommended conviction at acquisition + soft inside the app (sequential). The copy-conversion, behavioral-mechanism, and competitive-intel experts argued hybrid in every sentence — conviction noun + soft verb in the same line. **Resolution: hybrid-throughout is the right model. The "sequential" frame is what the founder reached for but the data on Aesop/Glossier and the cohort's anti-shame allergy says conviction noun + soft verb co-present always wins.**

**4. Pricing: $79.99 vs $89-99 vs cohort-tiered.**
Cohort-deep-dive said $79.99 to match MeAgain. Competitive-intel said $79-99. Behavioral-mechanism + contrarian wanted a $99 "Post-GLP-1 Maintenance" tier separate from $69-79 general. **Resolution: Ship $79.99 annual as the universal anchor for 30 days post-pivot. Then introduce a $99/yr "Maintenance" SKU for the post-discontinuation cohort once the maintenance curriculum is live. Hold $5.99 weekly for the on-GLP-1 financially-stressed cohort. Drop the founder's existing weekly diet-app signal.**

**5. Product changes — how aggressive.**
The behavioral-mechanism expert insisted progressive resistance must ship before any muscle claim (3-4 weeks). The copy-conversion expert said the positioning could ship first while resistance is in flight. **Resolution: the founder's pricing-and-positioning lift can ship in week 1-2 (App Store, paywall, cohort question, citations). Muscle claims and on-GLP-1-specific creative are gated behind the resistance track + injection-lite (week 6-8). Until then, conviction language attaches only to mechanisms the existing product CAN deliver (food noise CBT, satiety substitution, maintenance behaviors).**

A 6th smaller fault line: whether to keep "becoming" framing on the App Store. Copy-conversion said drop it from the hero; behavioral-mechanism + cohort-deep-dive said keep italic-Fraunces voice signal as the brand layer. **Resolution: drop "becoming" as the headline noun on the App Store; keep italic-Fraunces typographic signal as the JeniFit voice marker on accent words within conviction copy.**

### Kill triggers (when to abandon and retreat)

Pre-commit these so the founder doesn't re-decide at every dip:

1. **Phase 1 (week 2 review) — paywall conversion bar.** If trial→paid does not lift ≥30% within first 14 days post-Phase-1 (US cohort baseline 7-14% → must hit 10-18%+), the conviction-led GLP-1 positioning is not earning its weight. Retreat: revert App Store hero to "becoming" / "Quiet" register, keep cohort segmentation infrastructure for future use.

2. **App Store rejection on first submission.** If Apple rejects on 5.2.1 trademark or 5.1.1 health-claim grounds, allow 1 re-submission with softened copy. If 2 rejections, retreat to "Weight Care" only (drop "GLP-1 Era" from app name, keep in subtitle/description).

3. **TikTok ban rate >40% on UGC creator creative.** If 4+ of the first 10 creator posts get demoted/removed within 7 days, the cohort wedge cannot be reached on the founder's primary channel at viable CAC. Retreat: pivot acquisition to Apple Search Ads (long-tail GLP-1 keywords) + Reddit, and reset Phase 2/3 spending.

4. **30-day review rating drop ≥0.3 stars on the pivoted listing.** Bait-and-switch detection. Retreat: pull the muscle-preservation and "keep-it-off" claims until the substantiating product (Phase 2 curriculum, Phase 3 resistance) ships.

5. **Existing user complaint volume >2x baseline within 14 days of cohort question.** Brand-voice fracture signal. Retreat: make cohort question skippable / optional, soften paywall H1 toward existing register.

6. **FTC inquiry letter, state AG letter, or Apple medical-app heightened-review notice.** Hard stop. Pull all conviction-led claims to mechanism-only ("food noise," "satiety," "maintenance behaviors") until regulatory all-clear. This is the only kill trigger that requires immediate (same-day) rollback.

## "If founder is wrong, here's what" — pre-commit the pivot logic

The founder will be tempted to re-decide at every dip. Pre-commit these so identity doesn't override evidence.

**If at Day 21, conviction-led positioning + cohort segmentation produces <15% paywall conversion lift:**

The hypothesis "conviction-led GLP-1 positioning beats soft Quiet register at the App Store + paywall" is falsified for JeniFit's TikTok-acquired US cohort. Three possibilities, ranked by likelihood:

1. **The cohort mismatch is real (most likely).** TikTok-acquired 22-35 women under-overlap with the GLP-1 cohort. The Day 7 soft-survey already addresses this — if it showed <20% on/post/curious, you shouldn't have shipped Phase 1 in the first place. If it showed ≥20% but conversion still didn't lift, the acquisition channel is misaligned with the cohort. Retreat: revert App Store hero to soft register ("Quiet" or "becoming" wins), keep cohort segmentation infrastructure for future use, redirect $1500 toward Apple Search Ads on broader weight-loss keywords (testing whether ASA-acquired audience differs from TikTok-acquired).

2. **The product-mechanism gap is bigger than expected.** Cohort recognizes positioning but rejects product on first session. Retreat: pull all conviction claims, return to soft register, ship Phase 2 curriculum in stealth, retry conviction repositioning at Day 90 with substantiating product in place. This is the "fix mechanism first, then talk louder" path the behavioral-mechanism expert recommended.

3. **The hybrid register is mis-tuned.** Conviction is reading as too hard for the 22-35 cohort. Retreat: soften the App Store hero ("Weight Care for the GLP-1 Era" → "for the GLP-1 generation, the daily work that holds"), keep paywall H1 conviction-led, retry at Day 45.

**If at Day 21 results are mixed (15-30% lift, but ED-complaint volume spikes OR review rating drops):**

The conviction layer is converting but the brand-voice fracture is real. Action: introduce the "gentler mode" toggle 2 weeks earlier than planned, default ON for any user flagging ED-history or eating-disorder concern, soften the post-onboarding bridge screens, scale spend slower than planned. This is recoverable.

**If at Day 60, Phase 2 ships but maintenance curriculum has <50% completion:**

The post-GLP-1 cohort doesn't engage with structured 12-week curriculum. The "keep-it-off" wedge is real but the product format is wrong. Action: rework Phase 2 into ad-hoc lesson library (not curriculum) + Jeni daily check-in driven, drop the "12-week protocol" framing, retry. The cohort hypothesis stays; the product format pivots.

**If at any time FTC or state AG sends an inquiry letter, or Apple sends a medical-app heightened-review notice:**

Hard stop on all conviction-led claims same-day. Revert App Store + paid creative to mechanism-only language ("food noise," "satiety," "maintenance behaviors," "behavior layer"). Engage health-advertising counsel ($300-500/hr) within 48h. Do not respond to inquiry without legal review. The positioning may need to retreat to "Weight Care for women" with GLP-1 references removed entirely. This is the worst case and it's recoverable as long as you stop fast.

**If at Day 90, post-GLP-1 cohort + Phase 2/3 product all ship but blended CAC stays above $30 (current US tier rough breakeven):**

The cohort wedge is real but the unit economics don't support solo-founder scale at this acquisition cost. Action: shift to high-LTV, lower-volume strategy — introduce the $99 maintenance tier as the default, drop weekly tier entirely, narrow paid acquisition to lowest-CAC channel (likely ASA), accept slower growth, build organic SEO + content engine + creator partnerships as long-arc moats. The pivot succeeded as a positioning exercise but the business model needs the higher ARPU to clear.

**The founder's identity protection:**

If the pivot fails any of these triggers, it is NOT a personal failure of taste or judgment. It is data. The hypothesis was reasonable, the evidence said test it, the test resolved. The Round 1 positioning (Quiet + brand-voice-first) is still alive and the cohort segmentation infrastructure built in Phase 1 stays valuable regardless of which positioning wins. JeniFit at Day 90 with a tested positioning hypothesis and shipped infrastructure is in a stronger position than JeniFit at Day 0 regardless of the pivot's outcome.

The single most important pre-commit: **do not partially retreat.** If you trigger a kill, retreat fully and immediately. Half-pivots in both directions are the worst outcome — they dilute brand voice AND fail to convert.
