## §1 making real photos read premium (the matte system)

Morsel's dishes float because they're renders on white. Her photos have
restaurant tables, glare, half-eaten edges. The fix is never filters (food
honesty: show what she ate). It's FRAMING:

1. **tight plate crop** · center-square, zoomed so the plate fills ~85%.
   kills the messy table, keeps the imperfect food.
2. **uniform geometry** · one continuous corner radius (14pt) at every
   size. consistency reads as "catalog", not image quality.
3. **white matte** · 3pt paper-white inset + hairline stroke. the her75
   polaroid cue; turns 9 mismatched photos into a set.
4. **two sizes only** · day hero 168pt, supporting 76pt. no alternating
   collage (the article's own steering: alternating = cheesy).
5. **whitespace separates days, no shadows on photos** · zero card chrome
   in the food layer; air + type do all structure. the scrapbook offset
   shadow stays on the trend artifact only; the matte is the photos' chrome.

**Inline vs journal split:** the locked rule (v1.1 plan, decision 5: daily
kcal one tap deep) survives cleanly: the Becoming surface shows photos +
pattern language, ZERO kcal numerals; numerals begin inside the journal.
The deficit ask also fits: weekly counts on the surface, daily numbers deep.

## §5 the energy story (the honest deficit module)

The killed version was a DAILY gained-vs-spent verdict card. This version
counts WEEKLY accumulation and never marks a miss. Load-bearing rules:

- **vocabulary**: "deficit" is banned (post-Ozempic list, with burn/earn/
  crush). ships as **lighter days**: "days your body used more than it took
  in." spent = "your day used"; intake = "took in". status lines: "landed
  under, comfortably" · "close to even" · "landed a little over. tomorrow
  resets." the over state exists ONLY in the day detail, never colored.
- **gain-framed dots**: filled = lighter day. an over day, an under-logged
  day, and a not-yet day render as the SAME faint open dot (the one rule
  the rejected map donated). count reads "three so far", never "3 of 7":
  a denominator is a built-in failure count.
- **conservative gate**: a day fills only when the `kcalTotalHigh` sum <
  the spent-side floor. we claim under only when the whole uncertainty
  range is under; borderline says "close to even" and stays open. she can
  trust a filled dot completely; that trust is the feature.
- **coverage gate**: thin logging (< 2 plates and < ~800 kcal) = "not
  enough logged", neutral, counted neither way.
- **spent-side honesty**: Mifflin-St Jeor baseline from existing height/
  weight/age, + steps kcal + session minutes (MET estimate). breathwork
  contributes ZERO kcal (science-honest lock: cortisol, not fat-burn); the
  ledger names it anyway: "breathwork steadies the day. it counts in
  minutes, not calories."
- **placement**: below the steps|plates pair, above the journal teaser; the
  scroll reads causally: result (trend) → inputs (steps, plates) →
  mechanism (lighter days) → evidence (the plates). trend stays the only
  chromed artifact; lighter days is chrome-free dots + words.
