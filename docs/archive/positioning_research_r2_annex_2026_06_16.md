## glp1-cohort-deep-dive (conviction 7/10)

### Copy patterns

- **App Store hero (title + subtitle):** "JeniFit: After-the-Shot Method | the lifestyle that keeps your results"
  _Avoids Apple 5.2 trademark trap (no Ozempic/Wegovy in metadata). 'After-the-Shot' is the cohort signal that searchers use ('what happens after I stop'). 'Keeps your results' is conviction without claiming weight figures the FTC NextMed precedent killed._
- **App Store screenshot 1 headline:** "for the 64.8% who stop within a year — and want to keep what they earned."
  _The JAMA 2025 number is a publicly cited, defensible statistic. Naming the cohort by their literal situation (you stopped, or you're going to) is conviction-led without naming a drug. Reads as evidence not motivation._
- **Paywall H1:** "your weight stays. the food noise stays quiet. the habits do the work the shot used to do."
  _Mechanism-named (food noise is the cohort's own word per Reddit + PBS + WebMD), outcome-promised (weight stays, no number), brand-voice preserved (Fraunces italic on 'noise' and 'work'). This is conviction-then-soft: result first, identity second._
- **Onboarding screen 1:** "are you on a GLP-1, off one, or wondering? we built this for all three."
  _Three-way segmentation up-front signals 'we know exactly who you are' — the cohort's #1 complaint about generic apps (Medscape Reddit study). Conversion mechanic: branches the funnel into sub-cohort-specific copy from screen 2 onward. Soft register on the question, conviction on the next screen's branch promise._
- **Onboarding screen 2 (post-GLP-1 branch):** "8 in 10 regain. the ones who don't have a structure. this is the structure."
  _The Virta 8x stat (technically '8x more likely to keep losing with guided off-ramp') is publicly cited and defensible. Conviction-led, evidence-grounded, cohort-named. No drug names, no body-weight percentages, no FTC red flags._
- **TikTok ad hook (UGC creator script):** "month 9 off zepbound and i haven't gained. it's not willpower. it's this protocol. (screenshot)"
  _Creator-led, first-person, names the drug only in spoken/screen text (creator policy, not app metadata — protects Apple 5.2). 'Protocol' is the conviction word — Cal AI / MeAgain / Found all use it. Avoids TikTok's July 2025 weight-loss product ban because no body figures or before/afters._
- **Paywall sub-headline (after H1):** "high protein. food-noise retraining. a tapered off-ramp. the things the prescriber didn't have time to tell you."
  _Mechanism stack named explicitly (matches the joint ACLM/ASN/OMA/TOS 2025 nutritional priorities). The 'didn't have time to tell you' is the load-bearing insight — Reddit/Medscape repeatedly cite that prescribers prescribe and disappear. JeniFit owns the support gap._
- **Becoming tab hero (post-trial users):** "month 4. your *protein floor* held. your *off-ramp* is on track."
  _Personalized conviction using the user's own data (data provenance rule). Italic-Fraunces on the punch words preserves brand. Names the mechanism the user is winning at. This is where soft register earns its place — after the value has been proven._

  Why: The 64.8% discontinuation cohort isn't homogeneous — JAMA Jan 2025 + Virta show cost-quitters and side-effect-quitters and plateau-quitters need different next-step content. This makes the 'we know who you are' promise real and lets push notification copy be genuinely cohort-specific.

  Why: MeAgain (the actual cohort comp) is $79.99/yr — the cohort tolerates that. Weekly tier signals 'diet app' in this cohort and caps perceived value. - **Build a 'GLP-1 Curious' education track (5 lessons, JeniMethod-sequenced) — what GLP-1s do, who they help, who they don't, what behavioral protocols achieve a fraction of the effect, how to decide** _(effort: 1-2 weeks)_

- **Remove all 'AI' and brand-coined wellness verbs from screens that GLP-1 cohort sees first; add visible mechanism citations (small grey-text footnotes: 'per ACLM 2025 advisory', 'per JAMA 2025 cohort')** _(effort: 3 days)_
  Why: Cohort is hyper-skeptical of wellness fluff (Maintenance Phase, Burnt Toast register). Citation visibility is the cheapest conviction signal you can buy — it tells the post-Ozempic shopper 'this is the not-another-diet-app one.' Matches the existing voice rules (no 'AI') and adds an evidence layer Cal AI/MeAgain don't have.
- **Wire Apple App Store CPP (custom product pages) with 3 variants — On-GLP-1, Post-GLP-1, GLP-1-Curious — so paid creative across TikTok/Meta routes to a cohort-matched landing** _(effort: 4-5 days)_
  Why: The conviction copy only works if the creator video promising 'month 9 off Zepbound' lands on a screen that immediately repeats 'for the post-GLP-1 cohort.' CPP is the existing iOS feature for this and is free. Without CPP, all the segment work above leaks at the funnel join.

### Risks

- **[high]** Apple guideline 5.2.1 / 5.2.5 trademark rejection if Ozempic, Wegovy, Mounjaro, Zepbound, or Tirzepatide appear in App Store metadata (title, subtitle, keywords, screenshots) or in-app branding. Novo Nordisk filed 130+ trademark suits in 2025 and the existing GLP-1 apps that get away with it (Shotsy, MeAgain) use the marks with ® disclaimers and have been challenged repeatedly
  Mitigation: Use 'After-the-Shot' / 'GLP-1' (the receptor agonist class is generic) / 'the weight-loss shot' — never branded names. Treat Ozempic/Wegovy/Mounjaro/Zepbound the way you'd treat any registered TM: only in user-generated TikTok creator content where the brand is spoken by a person on the drug, never in app-controlled surfaces. Add a small in-app disclaimer ('not affiliated with Novo Nordisk or Eli Lilly').
- **[high]** FTC deceptive claims enforcement (NextMed $150k Dec 2025) — any quantified body-weight outcome ('lose 20 pounds', '23% of body weight') will draw scrutiny. The 8x stat from Virta and the 64.8% discontinuation from JAMA are citable; specific weight numbers in marketing are not
  Mitigation: All conviction copy uses cohort + mechanism + RELATIVE outcome only ('8x more likely to keep losing'), never absolute body-weight numbers. Cite the source visibly. Get a copy review pass from a regulatory-savvy reviewer before App Store / paid-creative submission. Avoid fake-testimonial pattern at all costs (it's what got NextMed).
- **[high]** Conviction-led repositioning collides with brand-voice memory rules ('post-Ozempic vocabulary', 'no crush/shred/burn/earn/deficit/AI', 'anti-femvertising', 'no fabricated stats'). If executed crudely, the repositioning torches the brand the founder spent 13 phases building
  Mitigation: Conviction is layered into OUTCOME claims ('weight stays', 'food noise stays quiet') and MECHANISM claims ('protein floor', 'off-ramp') — both compatible with the locked vocabulary. The italic-Fraunces voice signal works on conviction words ('*stays*', '*work*'). Hold the kill-list on labor verbs and explicit body-shame copy. Hybrid register is the only safe execution.
- **[critical]** Telehealth-bundle commoditization (Noom-Rx $100M ARR in 4mo, WW Med+ at 135k clinical members, Hims-Novo Mar 2026 settlement). If JeniFit positions head-on as 'GLP-1 weight loss app', the price comparison becomes JeniFit-$80/yr vs WW Med+ $25-74/mo-with-actual-drug, and the conviction-shopper picks the bundle
  Mitigation: Position EXPLICITLY against the bundles by owning the cohorts they CAN'T serve at the bundle price: the 64.8% who already quit, the cost-priced-out, the needle-averse, the curious-not-ready. App Store H1 should imply 'no prescription required' without saying it. Differentiation isn't 'cheaper Noom-Rx' (that's a loser) — it's 'the only app that fits when you're between, off, or instead'.
- **[high]** TikTok July 2025 weight-loss content moderation + GLP-1 patch crackdown + #SkinnyTok ban. Paid creative depicting before/after, body figures, or implied weight loss gets demoted or rejected. Founder's TikTok-acquired beginner-women cohort lives on TikTok
  Mitigation: All creator UGC is identity / process / non-figure framed ('here's what I do every morning post-Zepbound') not outcome-framed ('look at my body'). Creators speak the brand names; app metadata stays clean. Plan: 8-12 micro-creators at $200-500/post who post their actual maintenance protocol, not transformation reels. Build for the algorithm that survives the post-2025 moderation regime.
- **[medium]** Eating disorder activation among the Gen-Z women cohort. TimmermanReport Nov 2025 + the existing literature on GLP-1s and ED reactivation document a real harm pathway. Conviction-led 'weight stays off' copy on screen 1 of an app pulled by a 22-year-old at high ED risk is a brand-and-ethics problem, possibly an Apple safety review problem
  Mitigation: Keep the existing anti-shame copy rules and BMI-as-aware caption pattern. Add an onboarding age + ED-screen branch (existing onboarding has 'prior attempts' and 'food relationship' — these become triage signals). For users flagged at ED risk, downgrade conviction copy to soft register and surface JeniMethod CBT lessons first. This is the founder's strongest cultural advantage vs Cal AI; don't burn it for conversion.
- **[medium]** Solo-founder execution capacity — the product changes above add 7-9 weeks of work to a roadmap already managing v1.0.10 cleanup + a v2 strategy doc + content engine + onboarding v4.5 polish
  Mitigation: Sequence as 3 phases: Phase 1 (1 week) = positioning + paywall H1 + CPP + pricing reanchor + remove AI references. This alone tests the hypothesis. Phase 2 (2 weeks) = sub-cohort branching + Off-Ramp Protocol MVP using existing CBT lessons. Phase 3 (3-4 weeks) = full Off-Ramp Protocol + GLP-1 Curious track. Kill Phase 2/3 if Phase 1 doesn't show paywall-conversion lift in first 14 days.
