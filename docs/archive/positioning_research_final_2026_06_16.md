## Recommended Positioning Hierarchy

### PRIMARY: "Quiet" — Food Noise Companion (composite 8.0)

**One-liner:** "Quiet the food noise. Stop counting calories."

**Why this one:**
- 8 of 12 experts converge here
- Existing brand voice + JeniMethod CBT + breathwork + pre-eat mode + scrapbook = 80% of needed product already shipped
- Validates in 5-7 days with App Store CPP + paywall headline swap (cheap, reversible)
- Survives FTC/Apple/Meta regulatory floor (behavior claim, not drug-equivalence claim)
- Owns an unowned vocabulary for ~60-90 days
- Defensibility = mechanism (CBT curriculum + pre-eat decision + Becoming) not just claim
- Reads naturally to 22-35 cohort AND extends to 30-45 cohort

**Pricing under this positioning:** $79/yr annual + $29 quarterly default + $9.99/wk + 7-day trial on annual.

**Daily hero loop:** Morning lesson (4-7min CBT/ACT primer + implementation intention) → midday pre-eat decision (snap menu, get permission/tradeoff) → evening Becoming check-in (1-question noise score, identity reflection).

### BACKUP 1: "JeniMethod" — Curriculum-Led Program (composite 7.7)

**One-liner:** "A 60-day CBT-rooted program for women who want to lose weight without dieting again."

**Why backup:** Programs convert better than tools (Noom playbook). Same product, different commitment architecture. Higher pricing power ($59-79/yr or $29/quarter). Higher initial defensibility through commitment scaffolding. Tradeoff: longer to test (needs program-arc UI), conflicts with custom-duration-per-user lock, more user expectation management.

**When to switch:** If Quiet validates but you want to harden the commitment/retention story. Likely the destination 90-180 days after Quiet ships.

### BACKUP 2: Perimenopause Onboarding Branch (composite 7.3)

**One-liner:** "For women whose bodies stopped responding — perimenopause, post-Ozempic, and the 'I've tried everything' cohort."

**Why backup, not primary:** Highest LTV ceiling ($99-149/yr realistic). Capital validation (Midi $1B, Reverse Health $100M+ ARR). Brand voice DOES mature into this register with editorial restraint. BUT: founder credibility gap (you're a male solo dev — cohort wants female voice), CAC channel shift required (Mel Robbins/Pinterest/Facebook, not TikTok), brand voice migration is non-trivial.

**Ship as:** Cohort routing inside the Quiet primary — "Are you in perimenopause?" onboarding question routes to a peri-flavored paywall variant + customized lesson sequence. Free optionality on highest-LTV cohort without abandoning current audience.

**When to switch to primary:** If the peri branch outconverts the general Quiet branch by 2x at 14-day measurement AND you want to commit to brand-voice maturation as v2.

## Recommended Experiments

Each runnable in days. Each has explicit decision rule.

### Experiment 1: Food-Noise Positioning Smoke Test (5-7 days)

**Setup:**
- Ship 4 App Store Custom Product Pages:
  - "Quiet the food noise" (lead variant)
  - "Stop counting calories" (calorie-fatigue variant)
  - "Soft eating for soft girls" (Gen-Z voice variant)
  - Status quo "JeniFit" (control)
- New paywall H1 + new onboarding screen 1 for each
- Drive paid TikTok+IG split with 2-3 RD-credentialed creator partners ($1-3K each)

**Cost:** ~$3-5K paid + 3 days founder work
**Read window:** 7 days post-launch
**Decision rule:**
- US trial-to-paid >18% on any variant → commit, scale that creative
- US trial-to-paid 14-18% → iterate copy on top performer, run again
- US trial-to-paid <14% → escalate to Experiment 2 (audience reroute)

### Experiment 2: Cohort Routing via Onboarding Branch (7 days)

**Setup:** Add 2 onboarding questions:
- "Are you on, tapering, or recently stopped a GLP-1?"
- "Are you in or approaching perimenopause?"

Route YES on either to dedicated paywall variants. Run alongside Experiment 1.

**Cost:** 1 day founder work, $500 paid Meta to specifically-aged cohorts
**Read window:** 14 days
**Decision rule:**
- GLP-1 cohort or peri cohort converts >25% (2x general) → that cohort is the real wedge; pivot acquisition channel
- Both convert similarly to general → cohort isn't the lever, copy is

### Experiment 3: Hero Reorder — Lessons-First (3 days)

**Setup:** Rebuild Home so JeniMethod daily lesson is hero card. Demote workouts/food rails to "supporting" status. Ship to 50/50 of new users.

**Cost:** 2-3 days of engineering (UI re-rank, existing manifest)
**Read window:** 7-day cohort retention + 14-day trial conversion
**Decision rule:**
- D7 retention +20% vs control → commit to lessons-first
- Trial conversion +30% → commit AND raise pricing
- No lift → revisit; possibly lessons-as-PMF is engagement-vanity (the dissenting view), and the wedge is elsewhere

### Experiment 4: Pricing + Trial Length Test (7 days)

**Setup:** RevenueCat offering split:
- Control: $47.99/yr + 3-day trial
- Variant A: $79/yr + 7-day trial
- Variant B: $29/quarter as default + 3-day trial on annual
- Variant C: All three SKUs at new prices

**Cost:** Half-day of RC config
**Read window:** 14 days
**Decision rule:**
- Variant A or C net revenue per install higher than control → reprice all geos
- Variant B (quarterly default) converts higher AND retains 60-day rate >70% → make quarterly default
- US still at 7-14% on all variants → confirms positioning is the lock, not price

### Experiment 5: International Re-Targeting (14 days)

**Setup:** Apple Search Ads + Meta spend tilted to PH/SG/UK at $500/day cap. Localized CPP at slightly lower price anchor for those markets.

**Cost:** $7-10K paid spend over 14 days
**Read window:** 14 days
**Decision rule:**
- ROAS in PH/SG/UK >3x US baseline → confirm international primary acquisition for 90 days
- ROAS comparable → US-cohort problem isn't geo, it's positioning

### Experiment 6 (parallel research instrument): App Store CPP Lab

**Setup:** Use Apple's 70-CPP allowance to ship 6 positioning variants simultaneously. Per-page keyword indexing routes organic traffic to each.

**Cost:** 1-2 days of metadata + screenshot work
**Read window:** 14-21 days (organic discovery is slower)
**Decision rule:** Highest-converting CPP becomes default. This is research, not commit — let App Store data, not Slack opinion, pick the winner.

## If I'm Wrong About X, Here's What I'd Switch To

I'm holding three load-bearing assumptions. If any one of these breaks at the 30-day or 90-day mark, here's the pivot:

**If I'm wrong that "food noise" lands with 22-35 US TikTok cohort:**
The diagnosis would be that the vocabulary is too clinical/GLP-1-coded for the cohort. Switch to softer-frame variant: "soft eating" / "permission" / "becoming a steadier eater" — same product, different word. If "food noise" CPP underperforms control and softer-frame variants outperform, the wedge is anti-Cal-AI register, not specifically the noise vocabulary. Run another 14-day cycle on softer-frame variant. Don't escalate to perimenopause yet.

**If I'm wrong that the positioning fix is sufficient (i.e., the cohort itself is broken):**
The diagnosis would be Day 30 shows no lift on any variant in US 22-35 cohort. Then the contrarian-devil and venture-investor experts are right — the cohort itself is the leak. Hard escalate to perimenopause branch as PRIMARY, accept the brand-voice maturation cost (~60-90 days of design work), shift acquisition channel from TikTok to Meta/Pinterest/podcast. This is the bigger surgery but the data would have proven it necessary.

**If I'm wrong that lessons-first hero retains and converts:**
The diagnosis would be lesson-completion was engagement vanity on free-trial cohort (the behavioral-psych and contrarian dissent). Then the daily mechanic needs to be food-or-identity-trigger, not lesson-trigger. Promote pre-eat decision mode to hero, demote lessons to weekly curriculum side-rail. This keeps the Food Noise positioning intact but rebuilds the daily loop around the GoUnit decision moment.

**If all three are wrong (Day 30 + Day 60 both fail decisively):**
The contrarian case becomes operative. Best of bad options at that point:
- (a) Single-feature "Bite" app rebuild — strip JeniFit to pre-eat decision mode only, 60-day Cal-AI-killer sprint
- (b) Editorial Substack relaunch — JeniFit voice as brand, app retired
- (c) Hard perimenopause pivot with clinical advisor — bigger surgery, higher TAM

I'd rank in that order. (a) preserves engineering velocity, (b) preserves brand voice, (c) is the biggest swing.
