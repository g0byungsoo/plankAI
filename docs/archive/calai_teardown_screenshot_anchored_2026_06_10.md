## 2. Top conversion levers (ranked)

### S-tier (must-add for JeniFit)

#### Lever S1 — The two-step paywall (commit first, choose second)
- **Mechanism:** separates "yes vote on trial" from "which tier" — kills the cognitive double-load that causes 35%+ abandonment when both happen on one screen. Loss-aversion is amplified because the user already said yes before they see price tradeoffs.
- **Cal AI citations:** **calai40 → calai41 → calai43.** calai40 is the commit step (bell + "1" badge + No Payment Due Now + Continue for FREE — no tier choice visible). calai41 is the choose step (Monthly vs Yearly cards + timeline). calai43 reinforces the reminder promise.
- **Evidence:** Superwall 2026 documented +18-26% paywall-to-trial across 4 apps. Adapty 2026 confirms the same effect on H&F vertical.
- **JeniFit translation:**
  - **Step 1 (new):** italic-Fraunces "your *becoming* starts here ♥" + bell sticker with "1" badge (use existing coquette pack, NOT line-art) + "we'll text before anything changes" + ✓ no payment today + cocoa "continue" CTA + disclosure "$47.99/year — billed annually" + "already purchased?" text link
  - **Step 2 (existing paywall, condensed):** timeline + Annual / Quarterly / Weekly tier cards (Weekly de-emphasized as smaller pill below the two peer cards) + cocoa "continue" + same disclosure
- **Leverage rank:** **S** (highest-confidence single change in this brief)
- **Cost to build:** **medium** — touches `PaywallView` + RootView routing + entitlement state-machine

#### Lever S2 — Pace selector with live-result callout
- **Mechanism:** **agency transfer** (Bandura 1997 self-efficacy) — user picks own pace; locus of control shifts to her, driving post-trial adherence. Plus **anchoring** — "Recommended" pre-selected, flanked by Slow + Fast = Sunsteinian default-design. Plus **commitment device** — the Fast-pace warning operationalizes informed consent.
- **Cal AI citations:** **calai13 (Slow), calai14 (Recommended), calai15 (Fast)** — three positions of one slider with sloth/hamster/panther icons, live-updated "You will reach your goal in [4 months / 2 months / 25 days]" callout, daily calorie target ("2,130 / 1,689 / 918 cal"), and a red warning chip on Fast.
- **Evidence:** Cal AI's most-screenshotted onboarding pattern on Reddit (per `calai_research_culture.md` §5). Generates UGC. Pace selectors with live-update callouts lift commitment-phase conversion +12-18% (Adapty 2026).
- **JeniFit translation:**
  - 3 positions: **gentle / steady / focused** (post-Ozempic vocab; rejecting "Slow/Fast" connotations + animals)
  - Coquette glyphs: glossy snail (gentle) / glossy bunny (steady, pre-selected) / glossy bow-cheetah (focused) — uses existing 3D sticker pack
  - Live-update callout: "weeks to *becoming*: ~16 / ~12 / ~8" (italic-Fraunces on "becoming") + 1-line per-position reframe
  - **NO calorie number on this screen** — food rail hasn't normalized that mental model yet; revealing 918 cal here tips into Cal-AI-coded territory the cohort is resisting
  - "Focused" warning: italic-Fraunces "moves quickly — sleep + protein floor matter more ♥" (NOT red triangle — that's anti-shame violation)
- **Leverage rank:** **S** (highest-leverage question-level addition; also the screen most likely to get screenshotted to TikTok = organic acquisition flywheel)
- **Cost to build:** **medium** — new SwiftUI view, sticker assets, live-update math

#### Lever S3 — Three-stage loader with checklist (labor illusion)
- **Mechanism:** Buell & Norton 2011 HBS — perceived value scales with visible labor. Three screens (psychologically one continuous beat) means the loader is *acted*, not continuous, which lifts paywall-to-trial +9-15% (Adapty 2026 H&F). The checklist converts time into evidence.
- **Cal AI citations:** **calai33 (67%) → calai34 (91%) → calai35 (97%)** — same chrome, rotating sub-label ("Estimating your metabolic age" → "Finalizing results" → "Finalizing results"), checklist filling sequentially. Plus **calai31** as the completion-moment frame ("All done!" + finger-heart) and **calai32** which slots the ATT modal mid-loader at 21%.
- **Evidence:** Cal AI's own A/B (Superwall reference) — 3-screen vs 1-screen loader = +9-15% paywall→trial. Plus Adapty 2026: 17s median, optimal 15-22s; JeniFit's current 12s is below band.
- **JeniFit translation:**
  - Keep single view (don't re-introduce navigation pushes), but introduce **3 visible acts** in the sub-label rotation:
    - Act 1 (0–60%, ~7s): data ingestion — "factoring in your gentle pace ♥" / "tuning your eating window" / "matching your luteal week"
    - Act 2 (60–95%, ~3s): synthesis — "composing your becoming arc" / "weighing your projection curve"
    - Act 3 (95–100%, ~2s): completion bloom + italic-Fraunces "ready ♥"
  - Re-cap duration from 12s → **15s** (free +3-5pp conversion per Superwall 2026)
  - Checklist items already in JeniFit voice ("your *eating* story ♥ / cuisine match / calorie window / movement floor / your *becoming* arc") — keep, but rename "cuisine match" → "your taste fingerprint"
  - Mid-loader: ATT pre-prime at ~30% (pause progress) → iOS dialog → resume (calai32 pattern, JeniFit voice)
  - Completion: tap-to-continue with "see your plan" CTA (NOT auto-advance, per `calai_research_loader_expert.md` §10)
- **Leverage rank:** **S**
- **Cost to build:** **small-medium** — view structure largely exists; needs sub-label clustering + ATT integration + completion-frame tap

#### Lever S4 — Sign-in immediately before paywall ("save your progress")
- **Mechanism:** **sunk-cost lock + identity-purchase setup.** By the time the user invests 25+ screens, "save your progress" reframes any back-out as *losing* something concrete. Loss aversion is ~2× as powerful as gain framing (Kahneman). Plus: Apple Sign In authenticates the StoreKit purchase pipeline so payment happens in 1 tap.
- **Cal AI citations:** **calai39** (sign-in screen with Apple/Google/Email, placed *between* sources footer and paywall step 1).
- **Evidence:** Superwall 2026 across 6 apps — sign-in-pre-paywall lifts paywall-to-trial +12-19% AND trial-to-paid +14% on the signed-in cohort.
- **JeniFit translation:**
  - New screen between plan reveal and paywall step 1
  - Headline: italic-Fraunces "save your *becoming* ♥" (lowercase otherwise)
  - 3 buttons: Apple (cocoa, primary), Google (cream stroked), Email (cream stroked) — vertical stack with 60pt+ spacing per `calai_research_ux_designer.md` §10
  - Tertiary "maybe later" text link (NOT a sibling button) keeps the funnel open for the 12% who refuse
  - **Upgrade the existing Supabase anonymous account** — don't create new — preserves all onboarding data across auth boundary
- **Leverage rank:** **S**
- **Cost to build:** **medium** — touches AuthService + RootView routing; preserves the anon-first model

### A-tier (high-leverage adds)

#### Lever A1 — "Have you tried other apps?" sunk-cost activation Q
- **Mechanism:** **Cialdini commitment-consistency.** Answer doesn't gate anything; the act of answering positions the user as "someone shopping for the right app." Also: implicit competitive positioning without naming a competitor.
- **Cal AI citation:** **calai5** (Yes/No thumbs-up/thumbs-down).
- **Evidence:** Adapty 2026 — sunk-cost activation Qs at front of funnel lift trial start +5-9%. Mechanism is well-documented (Cialdini Influence, 1984).
- **JeniFit translation:**
  - "tried *everything* already?" (italic-Fraunces on "everything")
  - 3 options (NOT binary): "yes, a few times" / "yes, many times" / "this is my first real try"
  - Triggers downstream tone calibration — "yes many" path gets extra reciprocity copy in plan reveal; "first try" path softens the comparison frame
  - Drop thumbs-up/down (2018-coded per `calai_research_culture.md` §7)
- **Leverage rank:** **A**
- **Cost to build:** **small** — single new case in flow

#### Lever A2 — Date-target pill in plan reveal ("by May 18")
- **Mechanism:** **implementation intention (Gollwitzer 1999)** — specific date doubles goal-completion rate vs amorphous horizons. Specificity = perceived precision = perceived value (+11% paywall-to-trial per Adapty 2026 swap test).
- **Cal AI citations:** **calai36** ("You should lose: **5.3 kg by May 18**" in pale-lavender pill).
- **Evidence:** Gollwitzer's implementation-intentions meta-analysis; Adapty 2026 ranges→specifics swap test.
- **JeniFit translation:**
  - Add italic-Fraunces pill in plan reveal: "**~6 lbs by August 14 ♥**" (range + horizon, NOT explicit decimal — anti-shame compromise; date stays specific)
  - Pull date from user's pace selection × goal delta
  - Replace "Congratulations your custom plan is ready!" frame with locked "becoming, plotted." (already JeniFit) + this new date pill below
- **Leverage rank:** **A**
- **Cost to build:** **small** — copy + pill component already exists

#### Lever A3 — Reciprocity beat after vulnerability questions
- **Mechanism:** **Cialdini reciprocity.** After 6+ vulnerable questions (gender, weight, DOB, GLP-1, hormonal stage, food relationship), the brand acknowledges the labor of being honest. Trust compound on downstream Qs.

- **Evidence:** documented psychology since Mauss 1925; operationalized in onboarding by Headspace 2018 → Cal AI 2024. Lifts completion +3-5% per Adapty 2026.
- **JeniFit translation:**
  - Place between vulnerability cluster (GLP-1 / hormonal stage / emotional eating) and the calibration battery
  - Headline: "thank you for being honest ♥" (heart as terminal punctuation = signature)
  - Single 3D-glossy sticker (heart-locket OR finger-heart from coquette pack) on cream background — NOT the painterly halo Cal AI uses, NOT line-art
  - Sub: "this is how we tune the plan to *you*."
  - Single cocoa "continue" CTA
- **Leverage rank:** **A**
- **Cost to build:** **small**

#### Lever A4 — Notification pre-prime with mockup dialog (drop the pointing finger)
- **Mechanism:** **prime-with-preview** — pre-screen explains value exchange before iOS gives the binary. Apple's own 2024 dev session: +34% allow rate on average across iOS apps. Push-allowed users retain at +1.6× Day-7 (Braze 2026 H&F).

- **Evidence:** Apple dev session 2024, OneSignal 2023, Braze 2026 H&F — all confirm +22-58% range, median +34%.
- **JeniFit translation:**
  - Headline: "want a nudge from *jeni*? ♥" (italic-Fraunces on "jeni")
  - Sub: "one quiet one a day. nothing nagging."

- **Leverage rank:** **A**
- **Cost to build:** **small-medium** — mockup notification component is new

#### Lever A5 — "No Payment Due Now" safety strip + bell-with-1 trial-reminder
- **Mechanism:** **objection-resolution before objection-raising.** The single biggest objection in any trial-with-credit-card flow is "what if I forget and get charged?" Cal AI resolves it pre-emptively. The bell + red "1" badge mimics a real iOS notification (visual training).

  - Copy: "we'll send you a *quiet* reminder before your trial ends ♥" (italic-Fraunces on "quiet")
  - Above CTA: ✓ no payment today (lowercase, ✓ in cocoa)
- **Leverage rank:** **A**
- **Cost to build:** **small**
