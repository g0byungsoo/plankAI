# Becoming + Home redesign — 4 expert briefs

**Date:** 2026-06-06
**Trigger:** Founder reviewed the live Becoming tab and pushed back: *"this becoming screen looks so unnecessary. i get the point with magazine style to make it luxurious but in my personal opinion, ios app is ios app. it needs to be useful as a tool."*
**Goal:** Adversarial critique of the editorial direction shipped in
`aggressive_genz_luxury_2026_06_06.md` §3 (becoming masthead + "the june issue" + TOC + chapter pagination). Four POVs spawned in parallel. All four converged on the same core thesis — three with surgical agreement on what dies.

---

## Brief 1 — The Row (Mary-Kate + Ashley Olsen POV)

> The editorial chrome on this Becoming tab is cosplay. The Row doesn't put a masthead on a cashmere sweater. You've built a magazine wrapper around a tool a 24-year-old opens with one thumb between TikToks — and the first viewport gives her zero data and asks her to admire your art direction. That's not luxury. Luxury is removing the thing the customer didn't ask for.

**Kill day one:**
- Second masthead ("becoming" 32pt + "vol. xii ♥") — tab is already named Becoming
- "the june issue" eyebrow — phone tells her the month
- Entire TOC block — five chapter rows that don't navigate is the worst offender
- "vol. xii ♥" — voice-lock violation (hearts terminal-only) + vanity counter
- Hairline divider above eyebrow — separating chrome from chrome
- Subhero "you said you wanted to build the body you want. this is the proof." — thesis statement for an essay; user isn't here to read essays

**Keep:**
- Hero "you're / *becoming* steady." — the cashmere. One italic punch word, lowercase, adaptive copy pulled from her real data signal.
- Sunday Feature card with iridescent border — but only once it's the *only* ornament

**Redesigned top-of-scroll (5 elements):**
1. Hero: "you're / *becoming* steady."
2. One-line data anchor: "down 1.4 lbs this week. trend holding."
3. Sunday Feature card
4. Chapter I (your week) — data starts immediately, no eyebrow, no roman numeral
5. Chapters II–V flow, same treatment, no decorative numbering

**Principle:** *If you removed it and the user didn't notice, it was decoration — and decoration in a daily tool is contempt for the user's time.*

## Brief 2 — Miu Miu (one-charged-gesture POV)

> Restraint *with one beat of personality that signals the wearer is a person, not a brand manual.* The italic on "becoming" is that beat. It tells her — in the half-second before she reads the word — that this app was made by someone, for someone, with a point of view. Cal AI's hero is a calorie number in SF Pro. MFP's is a progress bar. Neither has a charged gesture. Both are tools. Tools lose to tools that are also a *feeling.*

**The one charged gesture to keep:** the hero italic. Fraunces italic on the punch word is the gold chain on the cashmere — carries voice, brand, and emotional register in two words.

**Cut, no hedging:**
- Second masthead (editorial cosplay; user not buying an issue)
- "the june issue" eyebrow
- IN THIS ISSUE TOC — page numbers that don't link are a lie; roman numerals on a fitness app are a costume
- Pink hearts sticker next to hero — violates the voice lock you wrote
- Sparkle stickers in Sunday card corners — iridescent border is already the charged gesture; two beats = one too many

**Redesigned top-of-scroll (4 elements):**
1. Hero: "you're / *becoming* steady." (no sticker)
2. Subhero (DM Sans textSecondary, generous line height) — *Miu Miu KEEPS this against The Row's verdict*
3. Hairline divider — one, quiet
4. Sunday Feature card — iridescent border earns its keep as the only decorated module; corner stickers cut

**Differentiator from The Row:** The Row says nothing — pure denial works for a 38-year-old creative director who knows who she is. Doesn't work for a 24-year-old who downloaded a fitness app at 11pm after a TikTok. Miu Miu says one thing, beautifully. **This cohort needs to be told one thing.**

## Brief 3 — Cal AI principles (utility-density POV)

> She didn't download a magazine, she downloaded a scale that talks back — every pixel either shows her the number or moves it.

**First-scroll redesign (7 elements):**
1. **Weight delta tile** — 1.4lb down, 14 days. EMA trend, 88pt numeric, sparkline. Tap = log weight sheet. Identity copy lives one line above in 13pt: *becoming steady.* (italic punch, no period). That's the entire editorial budget.
2. **Today's three rings, horizontal** — Plate (kcal-in trend, not target), Move (workout + steps merged), Recover (breath + sleep). Each ring tappable.
3. **"Log weight" CTA pill, full-width cocoa** — because weight logging is near-zero and it's the #1 input.
4. **Streak + next-session row** — "day 12 · next: lower body, 18 min." Tap = start.
5. **Goal pace projection, one line** — "on pace for goal week of aug 4" or "trending 0.3lb/wk — adjust pace?" tap-to-tune.
6. **Barrier-resolved counter, one line** — "you said evenings were hard. you've shown up 4 of last 7 evenings ♥"
7. **"this week" link** to the weekly recap surface.

Everything else (BMI card, plank mastery curve, Sunday Feature, WHO ring deep-dive) moves below the fold OR into the weekly recap.

**What this is costing:**
- **Time-to-first-action: catastrophic.** Cal AI first scroll = number + log button at <1s. JeniFit = 4-6s minimum to data comprehension.
- **Daily-open → log conversion** — weight logging near-zero is the smoking gun. Log action is buried.
- **Scroll-to-engagement** — TOC roman numerals don't anchor + page numbers don't navigate = broken affordance trains user that taps don't do things in this app. Bleeds into workout-start tap rate.
- **US trial conversion 7-14%** — Cal-AI-trained cohort opens JeniFit, sees magazine, pattern-matches "content app, not tool", churns before paywall.

**The compromise:** **Magazine dies on the daily dashboard. Magazine lives on the weekly recap.** Sunday 7pm push → "your week ♥" → dedicated surface where she ARRIVES having chosen reflection. THAT'S where editorial chrome (masthead, Sunday Feature, long-form barrier reflection, plank mastery curve, BMI banding) earns its scroll.

The daily Becoming tab becomes a dashboard. Voice locks intact; editorial chrome gone.

## Brief 4 — iOS UX + monetization expert (Gen-Z women, subscription)

**What this is doing to retention right now:**
- Cal AI cohort decides "tool or magazine?" in ~1.5s. Mobbin median first-screen dwell 2.3s. JeniFit's first viewport gives content-app signal → D1 retention drops to 35-40% range (Flo pre-2023 editorial mode) vs tool-app 60-70% (Cal AI, MFP, MacroFactor).
- TOC with fake page numbers = trust tax. Gen-Z TikTok-acquired users pattern-match "tappable but does nothing = fake app." Perceived-utility kill in <3s.
- 23% workout completion isn't a workout problem — Becoming teaches the app is for *reading about becoming*, then Home shows workout-as-hero. Register whiplash kills the next tap.
- Weight-logging near-zero is downstream — dashboard a metric whose daily-input ritual the app never prompts for in the first viewport of either tab.

**Reorg that moves trial conversion +30-50% relative:**
- Kill second masthead + "june issue" eyebrow + TOC (~340pt vertical of zero utility)
- Keep italic hero (one line, not two)
- ONE live data card with tap-to-act CTA: "log today's weight" or "log this morning's plate" with last-7-day mini-trend behind. (Cal AI's pre-vs-post home redesign Q3-2024 = +41% trial start by collapsing chrome.)
- Sunday Feature pulled up immediately under it.

**Keep from editorial:** italic-Fraunces punch word, lowercase casual, Sunday Feature as *weekly* recurring module (MacroFactor newsletter pattern). Chapter dividers stay BELOW fold as section headers, not as a TOC manifest. "vol. xii" register stays in end-of-month recap email, share-card export, post-paywall welcome screen. **Editorial is a retention frame, not an acquisition frame. You ran it as acquisition. Move it.**

**Home tab reorder (the loudest signal in the data — lesson 75% vs workout 23%):**
1. **Hero slot → JeniMethod lesson of the day.** Tappable, today's title, 2-min estimate, italic punch-word title. This becomes the primary daily ritual. Lessons clear 75% — retention engine.
2. **Health anchor slot → weight log + steps pulse, side-by-side.** Single-tap log. Last-7 trend behind number. Fixes weight-logging-near-zero by moving ask to highest-traffic surface.
3. **Utility nav → workout + breathwork as peers**, not workout-dominant. Breathwork clears 75%+ too — promote it. Workout becomes a *choice*, not assumption.
4. **Future rails → food camera, body scan, expanded JeniMethod library.**

Net: home reframes "do your workout" → "learn + log + choose your movement." Workout completion will likely go UP in absolute terms even losing hero slot, because users who choose it are higher-intent. Lesson + weight-log frequency carries D7. D7 carries trial conversion. Trial conversion carries the business.
