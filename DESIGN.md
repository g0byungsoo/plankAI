# plankAI Design System

Generated by /plan-design-review on 2026-04-22

## Visual Direction

**Palette:** BetterMe warm cream + Cal AI clean layout
**Font:** DM Sans (Google Fonts, free, all weights bundled in app)
**Tone:** Warm but irreverent. "Hey. Day 12 of 30. Don't blow it."
**Anti-patterns:** No pure blacks, no pure whites, no aggressive alarm colors. Everything lives in warm earth tones.

## Typography Scale

| Token   | Size | Weight       | LineHt | Tracking | Use                                              |
|---------|------|-------------|--------|----------|--------------------------------------------------|
| display | 56pt | 300 light   | 1.05   | -1.5%    | Timer (session), Core Score (post-session)         |
| title   | 32pt | 600 semibold| 1.15   | -1%      | Home greeting, paywall headline, Day 30 celebration|
| heading | 20pt | 600 semibold| 1.25   | -0.5%    | Section labels, exercise name on today's card      |
| body    | 16pt | 400 regular | 1.45   | 0%       | Quiz questions, card descriptions, sub-CTA text    |
| caption | 13pt | 500 medium  | 1.35   | 1%       | Day counter, "Billed weekly", metadata             |

Use-case anchors are binding. Timer MUST be display. Home greeting MUST be title.
No `.font(.system(size:))` calls in codebase. Use tokens or extend the scale.

## Spacing Scale (4pt base)

| Token | Value | Use                                        |
|-------|-------|--------------------------------------------|
| xs    | 4pt   | Within a component (icon + text gap)        |
| sm    | 8pt   | Between related elements (label + input)    |
| md    | 16pt  | Between content sections (default padding)  |
| lg    | 24pt  | Between distinct UI zones (card-to-card)    |
| xl    | 48pt  | Between major screen regions                |

Horizontal screen padding: always md (16pt). Cards: md internal padding.
Button tap areas: never < 44pt tall (Apple HIG).

## Corner Radius

| Token     | Value | Use                             |
|-----------|-------|---------------------------------|
| radius.sm | 8pt   | Inline elements, tags, pills    |
| radius.md | 14pt  | Cards, buttons, modals          |
| radius.lg | 24pt  | Hero cards, full-screen sheets  |

## Elevation

Single shadow style:
- Color: rgba(44, 34, 24, 0.08)
- Radius: 12
- Offset: x=0, y=2
- Applied only to bg.elevated surfaces (cards, modals)
- No shadows on buttons or CTAs

## Accessibility

- All text.primary on bg.primary: WCAG AA (4.5:1). #2C2218 on #F7F3EE passes at ~12:1.
- Minimum tap target: 44x44pt (Apple HIG)
- Dynamic Type: `.relative(to:)` on all fonts. Exception: display timer stays fixed at 56pt.
- Dark mode: out of scope for v1

## Onboarding Flow (25 screens)

### Zone A: Warm Welcome + Early Commitment (screens 1-5)
1. Welcome — "You're here. That's already harder than most people." Ref: BetterMe welcome
2. Goal — single-select: core strength, better posture, confidence, something else. Ref: CalAI goal
3. Plank experience — single-select: never tried, tried but gave up, do them sometimes, regularly. Ref: BetterMe experience
4. Current baseline — single-select: <15s, 15-30s, 30-60s, 60s+, not sure. (Skipped if "never tried"). Ref: Female Fitness
5. Barriers — multi-select: too boring, don't know what to do, no motivation, no time, injury concern. Ref: BetterMe barriers

### Zone B: Personalization + Education + Social Proof (screens 6-17)
6. Age range — single-select: 18-22, 23-27, 28-32, 33+
7. Activity level — single-select: sedentary, occasional, regular
8. Education — "Most people hold a plank for 20 seconds. Here's why."
9. Education — "Form matters more than time." Mini-illustration of hip sag
10. Education — "Bad form for 60s hurts more than good form for 20s."
11. Social proof — "Over X women started their Core Reset this month."
12. Testimonial 1 — tied to barriers selected earlier
13. Product education — "Your AI coach watches your form in real time."
14. Product education — "Hear coaching that keeps you honest."
15. Product education — "30 days to transform your core."
16. Testimonial 2 — before/after transformation
17. Commitment — "How many days this week?" single-select: 3/4/5/6/7

### Zone C: Close (screens 18-25)
18. Notifications — "Want a daily reminder?"
19. Notification time picker
20. Name input — "What should your coach call you?"
21. Voice preference — single-select: Encouraging / Balanced / Keep it real
22. Analyzing screen — progress bar, 3-5 seconds. Ref: CalAI analyzing (calai35.PNG)
23. Plan reveal — "Your 30-Day Core Reset is ready, [Name]." Ref: BetterMe program (betterme30.PNG)
24. Camera setup explainer — phone placement illustration
25. Paywall — 2-tier, yearly pre-selected. Ref: CalAI paywall (calai40.PNG)

### Flow Logic
- Linear. One branch: "never tried" on screen 3 skips screen 4.
- Back button on every screen except analyzing + paywall.
- Progress bar starts at screen 3 (avoid transactional feel on 1-2).

### Data Captured
- onboarding.goal, .experience, .baselineHoldSeconds, .barriers
- onboarding.ageRange, .activityLevel, .commitmentDaysPerWeek
- onboarding.notificationEnabled, .notificationTime
- onboarding.name, .voicePreference

## Session Screen Design

Audio-led session with minimal glance-check visual layer.

**Design principle:** The user should be able to complete a full plank without looking at the phone.

### Layout
1. **Camera feed** — fills screen, darkened (50% opacity overlay), desaturated. User's body ghosted.
2. **Pose overlay** — joint dots at moderate opacity. Color reflects form state:
   - GOOD_FORM: state.good (muted sage)
   - HIP_SAG / SHOULDER_CREEP: state.warn (warm amber)
   - NOT_IN_POSITION: muted gray
   - CAMERA_BAD: state.bad (muted brick) + text guidance
3. **Timer** — single dominant element. 60pt DM Sans Light, centered, slightly above mid-screen.
   Count-up (accumulation), not count-down. "47s" as personal-best-in-the-making.
   Below timer: "Day 1 · Standard Plank" in caption (13pt).
4. **No roast text on screen.** Roasts are audio-only. Post-session shows text.
5. **Bottom:** small "End session" with 300ms hold-to-confirm.
6. **Top-left:** audio on/off icon.
7. **Milestone flash:** 200ms warm cream wash on 30s/60s markers. Audio says the milestone.
8. **Final 10s:** timer pulses subtly (scale 1.0 → 1.05 over 500ms).

## First-Time Home (Day 1, pre-first-session)

Launchpad, not dashboard. Three jobs: pay off onboarding investment, preview 30-day arc, drive first session.

1. **Greeting** — "Welcome, [Name]. Your plan's ready." + goal-variant subhead. Title token.
2. **30-Day path** — vertical trail, 30 circular nodes, Day 1 active, unlock days (8/15/22) marked.
3. **Session card** — "Standard Plank", "Hold for 30 seconds", "~2 minutes", "Why this?" expandable.
4. **CTA** — "Start Your First Plank" bottom-anchored, full-width. bg.inverse on bg.primary.
5. **Sub-CTA** — "First, we'll help you set up your camera."

NOT on this screen: streak, Core Score, social proof, settings prominence.

## Empty State Philosophy

Empty states are never "no data yet" messages. They're always "here's what happens next" moments.

## Day 30 Celebration

Replaces normal post-session screen:
- "30 Days Done." headline (title token)
- Journey summary: starting hold → current hold, sessions completed, longest streak
- Hero roast quote from best-performing session
- Auto-generated Milestone Card for sharing
- CTA: "See what's next" → Continuous Mode home

## Continuous Mode Home (Day 31+)

- 30-day path transforms to week-based grid
- "Foundations Complete" tag, top-left, caption size, muted
- Session card continues with exercise rotation from unlocked pool
- Streak + Core Score continue
